# How To Cancel a  workload

***

 **1. Login to https://aac.amd.com/.**
    
   ![image](https://github.com/amddcgpuce/AMDAcceleratorCloudGuides/assets/137475062/d62dc96e-e37a-42b3-9b0e-72445014a621)

 **2. Launch a new workload. The User will be redirected to Workloads page. Click the newly launched workload.**

   ![image](https://github.com/amddcgpuce/AMDAcceleratorCloudGuides/assets/137475062/4cfe8529-e86e-45f1-9859-c219922d6b93)

 **3. Click 'Cancel Workload' button.** <br/>  
   **Note:** For interactive workloads like PyTorch, Tensorflow, Jammy etc, 'Finish Workload' button will be available instead of Cancel Workload.

   ![image](https://github.com/amddcgpuce/AMDAcceleratorCloudGuides/assets/137475062/c516ebac-e872-4511-bd71-17437dd21ddc)

 **4. User will be redirected to Workloads page. It can be observed that the workload status becomes 'Cancelled'.**
 
   ![image](https://github.com/amddcgpuce/AMDAcceleratorCloudGuides/assets/137475062/9b98f4ce-2bec-4f32-8bc5-537b9f648306)
